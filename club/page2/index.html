About Us

Our Mission

The Game Development Club is dedicated to fostering creativity, innovation, and technical skills in the exciting world of game development. We bring together passionate students who share a love for gaming and want to create their own interactive experiences.

What We Do

  • Weekly workshops on game design and development
  • Collaborative game projects and game jams
  • Industry guest speaker sessions
  • Participation in gaming competitions and conventions
  • Portfolio development and career guidance
